Welcome to our restaurant

In our seaside restaurant we source our ingredients carefully from local farms and suppliers. Our meny changes with the seasons and we always prioritize organic and ecological ingredients. We always serve both meat, fish and vegetarian options, with wine and other drinks sources from all around the world. Book your dinner package with our receptionist.

Our terrace bar overlooking the water serve artisanal cocktails paired with locally sourced snacks. We also have an extensive wine and beer menu. Ask our sommelier for suggestions.
