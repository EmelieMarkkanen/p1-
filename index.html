Welcome to Hemö

In the heart of the Stockholm archipelago Stockholm Spa Hotel is located on the island of Hemö. Here you will find curated experiences in our spa, restaurant and newly renovated terrace and bar overlooking the water.

Our spa offers cold and warm pools. We offer skin and massage treatments carefully selected by our experienced staff.

Our spacious rooms are furnished inspired by the soft color palette of the forest around the hotel. We also offer the chance to sleep under the stars in our glamping tent.
